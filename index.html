ABOUT SatsPEG

◆

What is SatsPEG?

SatsPEG is a collection of 8,000 unique pixel sigils living on Citrea, the first ZK-rollup on Bitcoin. Each sPEG represents a unit of satoshi liquidity crystallized into a distinct on-chain identity.

★

Why Citrea?

Citrea brings programmability to Bitcoin through zero-knowledge proofs. SatsPEG leverages this to create verifiable, Bitcoin-secured digital identities without leaving the BTC ecosystem.

♦

Rarity System

Six rarity tiers define each sigil: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legend, and Mystic. Rarity is determined at mint and cannot be changed.

∞

Ownership

Each SatsPEG is fully on-chain. No IPFS, no centralized servers. Your sigil exists as long as Bitcoin exists. True digital permanence secured by the most robust network in crypto.

HOW IT WORKS

01

Connect Your Wallet

Connect any EVM-compatible wallet (MetaMask, OKX, Rabby, Phantom, etc.) and switch to the Citrea Mainnet. The site will auto-prompt you to add the network if needed.

02

Choose Quantity

Select how many SatsPEG you want to mint (1-10 per transaction). Each mint randomly assigns a rarity tier to your sigil.

03

Mint Your Sigil

Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Your sPEG is generated on-chain and assigned a unique visual identity and rarity tier instantly.

04

Own Forever

Your SatsPEG lives fully on-chain on Citrea, secured by Bitcoin. Trade it, hold it, or use it as your on-chain identity across the ecosystem.

FAQ

What wallets are supported?

Any EVM-compatible wallet works with SatsPEG, including MetaMask, OKX Wallet, Rabby, Phantom (EVM mode), Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, and more.

What is the mint price?

Mint price will be announced before launch. The currency used is cBTC (Citrea's native token, pegged to BTC). Follow our Twitter for updates.

How is rarity determined?

Rarity is assigned randomly at the moment of minting using on-chain randomness. There are six tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legend, and Mystic. The distribution is weighted, with Mystic being the rarest.

What is Citrea?

Citrea is the first ZK-rollup that settles on Bitcoin. It inherits Bitcoin's security while enabling smart contracts and programmable assets. SatsPEG is one of the first NFT collections on this network.

Is there a max mint per wallet?

You can mint up to 10 SatsPEG per transaction. There may be a per-wallet cap announced closer to launch.

Where can I trade my SatsPEG?

After mint, SatsPEG will be tradeable on any NFT marketplace that supports the Citrea network. Details will be shared post-launch.
